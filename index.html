OneTab's Distinctive Feature Showcase

  • Optimized Tab Management When your browser is swamped with open tabs, hit the Merge Tabs button to unify them into a single, tidy list. Moreover, this feature allows you to restore individual tabs or the entire collection as needed effortlessly. By slashing the number of active tabs, you can free up to 95% of memory, dramatically enhancing your browser’s performance.
  • Midnight Mode Indulge in the elegant dark theme of the OneTab extension, providing a visually relaxing and comfortable browsing experience.
  • Effortlessly Name Your Tab Groups Click 'More' and select 'Name the tab group' to assign a custom name to your tab group. Furthermore, a box will appear, making it easy to label your group.
  • Pinned and Secured Tab Groups On your OneTab page, select 'More...' and click 'Star this tab group' to pin the group at the top of your list, highlighted with a star icon. Starred groups will be displayed in the sequence in which they were pinned. To further protect your tabs, choose 'Lock this tab group,' which will add a lock icon and prevent accidental deletion. Restoring tabs from a locked group ensures they remain in your OneTab list rather than being removed.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts Quickly access OneTab using the default shortcut: Alt + Shift + O (Display OneTab). To customize your shortcuts, visit chrome://extensions/shortcuts or right-click the OneTab icon, choose 'Manage Extensions,' click the triple-bar icon, and select 'Keyboard shortcuts.' You can also set up a shortcut to mimic clicking the blue OneTab icon, which sends all open tabs in the current window to OneTab. On the keyboard shortcuts settings screen, input your preferred shortcut in the 'Activate the extension' box.
  • Customization Panel Then, click the 'Options' link at the top of your OneTab page. This section lets you adjust pinned tabs, select the window for restored tabs, set up your startup preferences, and control duplicate tab handling. For further information, visit the settings screen.
  • Seamless Tab Reordering You can easily customize the order of your tabs in the OneTab list by dragging and dropping them to fit your preferred arrangement.
  • Tab Safety Assurance The Onetab Chrome extension ensures your tabs protection. Moreover, close all tabs, exit your browser, or restart your computer—OneTab will securely keep your stored tabs intact. Your data stays safe, providing continuous access to your saved tabs whenever needed.
